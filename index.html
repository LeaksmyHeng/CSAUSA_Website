Who We Are

We are Cambodian Student Association in the United States of America. Unlike a traditional student association. We are not belong to any school in particular, yet we are a group of students currently studying and locating in various parts of the United States.

We are supported and endorsed by the Royal Embassy of Cambodia to the United States of America. We are unique individules pursing to foster and empower all Cambodian students in the U.S.

We aim to create a plateform which all of us can connect and help each other accomplishing our dreams. "We Rise by Lifting Each Other".
