Leaksmy Heng

Director of Strategy and Business Development


Leaksmy is a graduate student at Northeastern University majoring in Computer Science. She has been working for Dell Technologies as a Software Engineer since 2020. Leaksmy earned her bachelor's degree from University of Massachusetts, Lowell in Finance and Management Information System. At Dell, she has been working on ETL (Extract, Transform and Load), tableau reports, and automation. Prior to joining Dell, Leaksmy was a member of the Manning Business and Entrepreneur Scholars in Training (BEST) program. She conducted a research on Exchange Traded Fund (ETF), miss pricing and arbitrage using STATA as the tool. Now that she is a part of CSAUSA, she hopes to foster a community where everyone is genuinely helping each other grow professionally, academically and personally.
Her favorite quote is "The greatest glory in living lies not in never falling, but in rising every time we fall." ~ Nelson Mandela
