Sokunannya Om

Chief of Staff


Sokunannya (Annya) is an undergraduate student in Accounting and Finance, with a concentration in Management Information System at Rutgers University. She is fluent in Khmer, English, and Mandarin. She is planning on getting her CPA (Certified Public Accountant) after her graduation in May 2022. While in school, Annya works as a research Assistant, teaching Assistant, and accounting tutor. In her free time, she likes playing board games and badminton. As a Chief of Staff in CSAUSA, Annya envisions this organization will help make scholarships in the U.S.A easily accessible for all Cambodian students.
Her favorite quote is “ I’m a great believer in luck, and I find the harder I work, the more I have of it.” ~ Thomas Jefferson, former President of the United States
