Vannikathara Dee (Kimmy)

Director of Digital Marketing and Public Relations


Vannikathara (Kimmy) is an MBA student at Western Governors University. She is currently a web application software engineer at Dallas College. She has 7+ years of industry experience in the Information Technology (IT) field. Work experience includes facilitating developer and customer communication, providing servant leadership of agile teams, organizing and leading projects or events. She is a Certified Scrum Master (CSM) and ITIL Foundation Certified. She is very passionate about technology and enjoys problem solving. With a background in software engineering, she wants to inspire and encourage young women to pursue careers in technology and engineering. On that note, as one of the co-founder of CSAUSA, Kimmy wants to empower young Cambodians to pursue higher education abroad, and hoping that CSAUSA will be the right platform and right venue for them.
Her favorite quote is “Brick by Brick We Build a House.” ~ Racoon
