Disovankiri Boung

President


Disovankiri (kiri) graduated from George Washington University with a master degree in Applied Economics. He received his bachelor's degree from Japan, and did one-year oversea exchange in the UK. His background is in business administration, hence he is familiar with various topics such as finance, marketing and consulting. He also participated in a few business case competitions and had a chance to coach some freshmen on communication and critical-thinking skills. He has now been working for Coherent Economics as research analyst. As a co-founder of CSAUSA, Kiri wants to make all Cambodian students studying in the USA feel like home and help them to achieve their academic and professional goals. He said "CSAUSA provide a platform for me to interact and learn from peers."
His favorite quote is “ Everyone has the Capability to Succeed.” ~ Ceteris Paribus
