CSAUSA Membership Information

Membership is written to the specification laid out in Article-II of our constitution .

Section 1: All students, who are or have been studying in the US regardless of their socioeconomic status are eligible to become a member of CSAUSA.

Section 2: All members of the CSAUSA are eligible to vote and nominate officers unless there is a specific requirement(s) for the office position.

Section 3: Application is required to become a full member of the CSAUSA. The application will be invalid if there is missing information.

Section 4: Membership shall be revoked if a member violates the rules and regulations of CSAUSA or the law of the Royal Government of Cambodia or the United States . No members shall be removed permanently from the CSAUSA without a statement from the defendant.
